You have entered the ZOMBIE MOUNTAIN!
This mountain is filled with zombies!
They are blind but really dangerous. 
They can feel your scent and come after you!
You have to move fast and attack swiftly!
The more agility you have the less damage the zombies
will do to you or even miss you!
To avoid the attack and destroy the zombie you will
have to press a certain key as fast as you can!
Press any key to start when you are ready!
